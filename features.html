Scanning

Scan any puzzle box in seconds

Open the scanner, point it at the barcode on any puzzle box, and PuzzleBee identifies it instantly. Title, brand, piece count, difficulty, and box art — all pulled in automatically.

If the puzzle isn't in our database yet, you can submit it with photos. Once approved, it's available to every PuzzleBee user.
