App descriptions

Short — 1 sentence

PuzzleBee is the jigsaw puzzle app for collectors: scan boxes, track your collection, time your solves, and share with friends.

Medium — 2–3 sentences

PuzzleBee is a jigsaw puzzle companion app for serious collectors. Scan any puzzle box barcode to add it to your library, use the built-in multi-session timer to track your solve time, and share finishes with friends in the Hive — a quiet, pressure-free social feed. Free to download, no subscription required.

Long — full App Store description

PuzzleBee is the puzzle app collectors have been waiting for. Whether you have 10 puzzles or 500, PuzzleBee brings order to your collection and joy to every solve.

Scan any puzzle box. Point your camera at the barcode and PuzzleBee identifies the puzzle instantly — title, brand, piece count, difficulty, and box art pulled in automatically. If a puzzle isn't in our database yet, you can submit it for review.

Track your collection. Every puzzle you own lives in one place. Mark puzzles as on the shelf, in progress, or complete. Filter by status. See your history at a glance.

Time every solve. Real puzzle solves happen across multiple sittings. Start the timer, pause it, come back — PuzzleBee accumulates an accurate total. When you finish, see how your time compares to the community average.

Share with the hive. Follow the friends you puzzle with. The Hive feed shows what they're solving and when they finish. No likes, no comments — just the quiet satisfaction of knowing your friend just finished a 1000-piece at midnight.

Stay motivated. Set a yearly puzzle goal and keep your daily solving streak going. Earn achievements that actually feel meaningful.

Free to download. No subscription. No ads. Just a better way to live your puzzle life.
