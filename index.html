How it works

From box to solved in five steps

1

Scan the barcode

Open the scanner and point it at the box. PuzzleBee matches the UPC against its database and pulls in the title, brand, piece count, and art.

2

Add to your collection

One tap adds it to your shelf. If the puzzle isn't in the database yet, you can submit it — photos and all — for review.

3

Start a solve session

When you sit down to puzzle, start the built-in timer. Pause it. Come back. PuzzleBee keeps an accurate total across as many sessions as the puzzle takes you.

4

Mark it solved

Hit complete when the last piece drops. Your time is logged, your streak is updated, and your stats get smarter.

5

Share with the hive

Friends who follow you see your finish in their Hive feed. No pressure — just a quiet, warm acknowledgment that you did the thing.
